The Mill Code School - Website Project

1Fan.co

I was lucky enough to participate in gener8tor's gAlpha Indy 5G venture creation workshop during May 2020. As a part of the program we were tasked with ideating and building potential tech solutions within many different industries across the world. From the first meeting and throughout the following weeks an idea kept resonating with me within the sports and event industry. I looked at a gap in the fan experience. Thru my recent experiences of attending my favorite sporting event along with the gaps or challenges I had that could have been improved, guided, or eliminated altogether. Through the workshop and continuous conversations with the gAlpha team I pursued the idea that ultimately turned into 1Fan. Click the link to learn more!
